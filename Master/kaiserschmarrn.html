Recipe: Kaiserschmarrn

Description

A fluffy german pancake-like food product, as adjusted to my partner's taste.

Ingredients

  • 4 large eggs (separated)
  • 1 pinch salt
  • 0.5 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1 5/8 cups milk
  • 1 cup flour
  • 1-2 tablespoons butter

Steps

  1. In a medium bowl, beat the egg whites to soft peaks, adding a pinch of salt toward the end.
  2. In a separate large bowl, beat the egg yolks and vanilla until the mixture is light yellow.
  3. Add the milk and flour–a little at a time to avoid lumps–and blend well.
  4. Fold in the egg whites and let the batter rest for about 10 minutes.
  5. Melt 1 to 2 tablespoons of butter in a 10- to 12-inch frying pan.
  6. Stir the batter again gently and pour it into the hot pan.
  7. Cover the pan and let the pancake cook for 10 minutes over medium heat. Flip over and cook about 10 minutes more; you might have to cut it into several portions to flip.
  8. Cut or pull the pancake apart into bite-size pieces while it continues to cook.
  9. When it is browned a bit, it is ready to serve.
  10. If you like a chewier consistency, let the pancake cool down a bit.
