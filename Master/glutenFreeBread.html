Recipe: Gluten Free Bread

Description

I could put a whole story here, about how I came to be making gluten free bread, but I can't imagine that anyone ever reads those walls of text. I never do. It's a recipe, make, eat, be happy.

Ingredients

  • 1.5 cups warm water
  • 2 eggs
  • 1.5 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • 1 teaspoon cider vinegar
  • 2.5 cups gluten free all-purpose baking flour
  • 2 teaspoons xanthan gum
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 tablespoon white sugar (sub: dextrose)
  • 1 tablespoon active dry yeast
  • 1/32nd teaspoon calcium propionate (preservative)
  • 1/16th teaspoon ascorbic acid (vitamin c)
